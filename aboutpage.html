ABOUT ME

My name is Sayed Ahmed; I am one of ten kids. I was born in a small village in Bangladesh. My family and I immigrated to the US in 2005. Since I was in kindergarten to now I have been drawing and I have always loved drawing. I have this passion for art and there is this inner feeling that wants to pursue it and can not live without it.

I am currently attending college working on an associate’s degree in graphic design. I am studying graphic design because I love the use of technology and art. I have been playing with digital art since I was in kindergarten from drawing in Microsoft paint, working on my editing skills in pixlr.com, to eventually using Photoshop, then learning illustrator, and now Premiere Pro and InDesign.

In the future, I hope to transfer to a university, have an internship in a design company, and eventually work for a studio. One thing I am certain of is I’ll still be creating art and finding ways to express myself in whatever way I can.
